AIICO Insurance offers two distinct annuity options to provide guaranteed regular income for life: the Immediate Annuity Plan and the Deferred Annuity Plan. [1] 
The exhaustive plan features, structural mechanics, and payout structures for both options are detailed below. [2] 
------------------------------
## 1. AIICO Immediate Annuity Plan
This plan converts a lump-sum payment into an immediate, consistent stream of retirement income. It can be funded directly by personal savings or by converting a Retirement Savings Account (RSA) balance through the PenCom Retiree Life Annuity framework. [1, 3, 4] 

* Funding Mechanism: Requires a single, lump-sum premium payment at inception.
* Payout Timing: Regular earnings begin immediately following the premium deposit.
* Income Frequency: The annuitant can choose to receive payments monthly or quarterly.
* Spousal Security: Includes a built-in joint-life provision allowing a surviving spouse to continue receiving 75% of the annuity benefit after the primary annuitant passes away.
* Lifelong Guarantee: Payouts act like a regular salary and are guaranteed for the remainder of the policyholder's natural life. [3, 4, 5, 6, 7] 

------------------------------
## 2. AIICO Deferred Annuity Plan
This plan functions as a long-term retirement savings vehicle, allowing individuals to build up capital during their active working years to fund a guaranteed income stream that starts at a designated future date. [1, 4] 

* Funding Mechanism: Supported by flexible premium options, allowing payments via check or direct debit on a quarterly, bi-annual, or annual basis. [5] 
* Payout Timing: Income disbursements are delayed until a future retirement date chosen by the policyholder. [1, 4] 
* Financial Control: Policyholders pre-determine their target retirement earnings and adjust their savings accumulation schedule accordingly. [1] 
* Death Benefit: If the policyholder passes away during the accumulation phase, beneficiaries receive a guaranteed payout equal to 5 times the annual earnings. [1, 3] 
* Tax Efficiency: Contributions grow on a tax-deferred basis, maximizing compound growth until retirement. [4, 8] 

------------------------------

The Stanbic IBTC Insurance Retiree Life Annuity is a structured, long-term insurance contract designed strictly under the guidelines of the Pension Reform Act of 2014. It allows you to convert a lump-sum premium—either from your Retirement Savings Account (RSA) or personal cash savings—into a guaranteed stream of tax-free income for the rest of your life. [1, 2, 3] 
The full breakdown of the plan’s features, payout structures, and operational rules includes:
## 1. Two Main Payout Options
When setting up your contract, you can choose how your periodic income is managed over time: [2, 3, 4, 5] 

* Level Income Payment Option: This pays out the highest possible starting amount. The payment remains exactly the same every month or quarter for your entire life, regardless of economic inflation. [1, 2] 
* Increasing Income Payment Option: This functions as an inflation hedge. Your regular payment will automatically increase annually by either 5% or 10% depending on your initial selection. [2, 3, 4] 

## 2. Payment Frequency & Commencements

* Frequency: You can choose to receive your payouts on a monthly or quarterly basis.
* Commencement Timeframe: Payments begin immediately after Stanbic IBTC Insurance receives the premium from your Pension Fund Administrator (PFA). If funds arrive before the 15th day of the month, your very first annuity salary is paid out within that same month. [1, 6] 

## 3. Estate Planning and Family Protection
The policy includes a built-in safety net for your designated beneficiaries to ensure your capital is not lost if you pass away early: [1, 3, 7, 8] 

* RSA Funds Guarantee: If funded via an RSA, the plan carries a strict 10-year guaranteed period. [2] 
* Early Passing Protection: If you pass away within the first 10 years, the remaining balance of the annuity fund for that 10-year window is paid directly to your named beneficiaries. [3, 7] 
* Outliving the Guarantee: If you live past the 10-year mark, your personal payouts continue uninterrupted for the rest of your life. (If you die after the 10-year mark, no residual balance is transferred to beneficiaries). [7, 9] 
* Personal Cash Funds Guarantee: If you fund the plan via non-pension, personal cash savings, you have the flexibility to customize the guaranteed period anywhere from 0 to 20 years. [2] 

## 4. Financial & Regulatory Benefits

* Tax Exemption: In full compliance with federal tax guidelines, the premium you pay and all subsequent retirement income you receive are 100% tax-free. [2, 3] 
* Market Protection: The risk of market crashes or investment losses is shifted entirely onto [Stanbic IBTC Insurance](https://www.stanbicibtcinsurance.com/). Your payout figures are locked in and guaranteed by contract. [1, 3, 5, 9, 10] 

## 5. Sign-up Requirements
To draft an official [Stanbic IBTC Annuity Agreement](https://www.stanbicibtcinsurance.com/static_file/Nigeria/nigeriainsurancelimited/Downloads/PDFs/FAQ+for+Annuity.pdf), you must provide: [6, 11] 

   1. A standard PFA official statement of account (less than 30 days old).
   2. Your Bank Verification Number (BVN).
   3. A valid Government ID (National ID, Passport, Driver’s License, or Voter's Card).
   4. Passport photographs of yourself and one for each named beneficiary.
   5. Your official Employer Retirement Letter.
   6. A recent utility bill (less than 6 months old). [3, 10, 11]

## 1. Two Streams of Funding
You can enter into the plan using two distinct channels depending on your current career stage:

* Immediate Option (Pension Lump-Sum): Tailored for prospective retirees who transfer the accumulated balance of their Retirement Savings Account (RSA) through their PFA directly into SanlamAllianz upon retirement. [1] 
* Deferred Option (Personal Accumulation): If you choose to save outside the statutory pension framework, you can use their Personal Retirement Plan to make flexible regular contributions (minimum of ₦10,000 monthly). ## 2. Core Payout Structures
SanlamAllianz designs the regular payout streams to suit your specific lifestyle and macroeconomic goals: [10] 

* Flat Rate Income Option: Provides a higher initial monthly or quarterly salary that remains completely stable over time.
* Inflation-Protected Option: Starts with a slightly lower baseline but features an escalating percentage adjustment every year to shield your purchasing power from inflation. [2, 10] 

## 3. Family Protection and Dependents Cover

* 10-Year Guaranteed Window: In compliance with NAICOM and PenCom regulations, the plan guarantees payments for a minimum of 10 years. If you pass away within 10 years of purchasing the plan, your named beneficiaries receive the exact valuation of the remaining payments for that 10-year block. [2, 10, 11] 
* Lifetime Continuity: If you outlive the 10-year mark, you continue to receive your payouts until the end of your natural life. [10] 
* Dependent Rider Option: You can choose a joint/dependent setup. If the principal annuitant passes away, a regular income stream transfers to a selected dependent or spouse for their continued livelihood. [1, 2, 10] 

## 4. Legal Compliance and Benefits

* Tax Shield: All funds processed through the GLRP are fully exempt from taxes under federal guidelines.
* Regulatory Alignment: Fully governed under Section 4.0 Rules of General Application of the revised Retiree Life Annuity (RLA) regulations in Nigeria. [10, 12]
